About Us

The Portland Hacker Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to supporting the hacker community in Portland, Oregon. We provide grants, resources, and support for hacker projects, events, and initiatives.

Our Programs

Grants

We provide financial support for hacker projects, events, and initiatives in the Portland area.

Events

We organize and support hacker events, workshops, and meetups in the Portland area.

Resources

We provide resources and support for hackers and hacker spaces in the Portland area.
